How organizations can incorporate UN SDGs?
Organizations can use a 3 steps process to incorporate UN SDGs in their strategy
1. Identify and prioritize the most relevant SDGs for your organization
2. Set concrete goals and develop KPIs to measure and monitor progress.
3. Capture your results, process and ambitions in a dashboard or report
that you share with internal and/or external stakeholders.
